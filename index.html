Who We Are

Zery Engineering Company LTD

Zery Engineering Company Limited is a fully owned Nigerian EPICMMT (Engineering, Procurement, Installation, Commissioning, Maintenance, Manpower Placement and Training) company engaged in Instrumentation and Controls, Integrated Safety Systems, Industrial Electrical, Process Automation and Control, Public Address and General Alarm System, Fire & Gas Detection and Cathodic Protection. We are the Distributor/OEM Representative of Unitronics PLC (Manufacturer of unique Programmable Logic Controllers plus HMI, VFD and AC Servo Drives) Katronic Ultrasonic (manufacturer of Ultrasonic Flow Meters) and UWT Level Control (manufacturer of different types of Level Transmitters) in Nigeria.
