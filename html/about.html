About Us

ZERY ENGINEERING COMPANY LTD

ZERY ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (C.A.C.) of Nigeria as a limited liability company. It was incorporated on the 19th of April, 2007 in accordance and compliance with the Companies and Allied Matters Decree 1990 of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, West Africa with registration number: RC 688412.
ZERY ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED has very handy human and material resources which exist in form of: Infrastructure, Finance, Working Environment, Certified Skilled Personnel, Technical Know-How and Established Quality Policy & Objectives. We have a large pool of highly technical, practical and professionally competent personnel. Our consultants are more than seasoned, with many years of working experiences. Our administrative, technical and field personnel have not only wide expertise, but have worked as a team long enough to guarantee fluidity of operation and success.
The management team of ZERY ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITEDis well focused in prompt execution and quick provision of efficient and optimal services for its valued clients. Our well mapped—out procedures and strategies for efficient job execution are backed up with properly guided policies and objectives, which are strictly adhered to. Also, our management team is geared towards continual improvement in in standard, professional-ethics and integrity in contracting, servicing, consulting, training and procurement in energy and oil field services.

Odewusi David T.O CEO Zery Engineering
